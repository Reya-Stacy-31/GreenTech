Privacy & Security

Your Data is Protected

We take your privacy seriously at Greentech. Your personal information and gardening journey are safe with us.

What we collect:
• Name, email, and phone number
• Garden photos and plant progress
• Location (city only) for local community features
• Gardening activity and badges

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your gardening experience and recommendations
  • To show relevant community posts and nearby nurseries
  • To award badges and track your progress
  • To send important updates about your plants
  • To improve the app based on usage (anonymously)

Security Features

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure Firebase backend
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Regular security audits
  • No selling of personal data to third parties
